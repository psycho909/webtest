The Origin of Darts

Originating from England in the 15th century, darts is a popular indoor sport which is popular in some countries and regions in Asia, Europe, America and Japan. It has been known that the darts and wine/beer culture are inseparable. In these places, darts has become a new trend with people of all ages and ethnicity enjoying the sport.
